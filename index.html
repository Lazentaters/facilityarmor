About Facility Armor

Company Overview:


Established in November of 2018, Facility Armor is a manufacturer and wholesale distributor of high-end specialty floor protection products, with the main focuses being custom entrance matting and gym floor protection. We offer custom products, but with simplified pricing structures, and quicker turn-around time than our competitors have on in-stock standard items. Our goal is to quickly become THE leader in the market through product innovation, unequaled service, and a commitment to providing our customers with the highest quality and highest value floor protection products in the industry.

Company Values:


Facility Armor is an employee centered company. We believe that through open communication we can provide a rewarding, safe, and fun work environment with opportunities for growth. That belief is grounded in the simple guiding principle that well supported and inspired employees produce well supported and inspired customer relationships.
